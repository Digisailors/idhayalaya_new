About Idhayalaya

IDHAYALAYA always strives for the best.

Idhayalaya heart foundation is the cardiac unit of Sundaram Arulraj Hospital. The Cardiology block of Idhayalaya was innagurated on 13th November 2005. Dr.Neelambujan , M.D, DNB(cardio) , in the Chief interventional Cardiologist and Director of Idhayalaya heart foundation. Dr.T.Neelambujan completed the post doctoral qualification in cardiology(DNB) from the world renowned Sri Sathya Sai institute of higher medical sciences puttaparthi , Andhra Pradesh in the year 2000 . Dr.Neelambujan has the credit of being the first person to initiate cardiovascular interventional in southern Tamilnadu in 2005. Ever since its inception the cardiac cathetarisation lab has performed many thousands of interventional procedures with successful results.
